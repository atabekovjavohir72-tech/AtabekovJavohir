The Godfather

Vito Corleone The head of the Corleone family, is a powerful and influential figure in the world of organized crime. He is known for his calm demeanor, strategic thinking, and unwavering loyalty to his family. Vito's character is portrayed by Marlon Brando in the film adaptation of "The Godfather."

Michael CorleoneThe youngest son of Vito Corleone, is a complex and multifaceted character. Initially reluctant to get involved in the family business, Michael eventually becomes deeply entrenched in the world of organized crime. He is known for his intelligence, strategic thinking, and ruthless nature. Michael's character is portrayed by Al Pacino in the film adaptation of "The Godfather."

Tom Hagen The adopted son of Vito Corleone, serves as the family's consigliere, or advisor. He is a calm and level-headed individual who provides valuable counsel to the Corleone family. Tom is known for his loyalty and dedication to the family, as well as his legal expertise. Tom's character is portrayed by Robert Duvall in the film adaptation of "The Godfather."
